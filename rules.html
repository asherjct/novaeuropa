Server Rules

On Nova Europa MC we want everyone to have the best experience possible. There are some rules and limitations to help keep everything fair for everyone. Our server rules may be found below. Please remember that our rules are not limited to everything listed below! Use good common sense and you will be fine.

  • No griefing or stealing from other players.
  • No cheating or use of any Hack Client.
  • No bug exploiting.
  • No spamming or advertising other servers.
  • No use of X-Ray.
  • Use appropriate language; no offensive or harmful speech.
  • Respect all players and staff members.
  • Report any bugs or issues to the staff or discord server.
  • Enjoy and have fun!

Players found breaking these rules can expect a suspension of chat privileges or access to the Nova Europa MC Server and Discord. Our rules are here to maintain a welcoming and safe environment for all players.
