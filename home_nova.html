About Nova Europa

Nova Europa is a simple, welcoming and down to earth faction server. We use plugins such as shop and slimefun to make our players experiences more immersive and realistic. In Europa you can build, pvp, do redstone, and so much more to your hearts content! We hope you have a great time on our server!

Information to Start

  • You are able to set 1 home using /sethome
  • You can create a faction using /f create, it costs 100 in game $
  • You can create a settlement by creating a ticket and telling staff where the settlement is to be located and what it is to be named, please join our discord for more info
  • To make money you can sell items in the shop (/shop)
